## 2. Historical Benchmarks and Brand Origins
The database maintains a chronological record of brand establishment, allowing for precise historical identification. According to our records, **Barq's** is identified as the root beer brand with the longest history, serving as the oldest entry in the Beer Factory dataset. Our logs confirm that Barq’s is a bottled brand with an inaugural brewing year no later than 1930. Conversely, **River City** holds the record for the shortest brewed history among all cataloged brands.

Regarding historical density between 1996 and 2000, we have documented three primary first-brew dates. Listed from latest to earliest, these are **Sparky's Fresh Draft**, followed by **Bulldog**, and finally **Captain Eli's**. 

## 3. Global and Domestic Brewery Infrastructure
The geographic distribution of our source breweries is heavily weighted toward the Western Hemisphere. Our registry catalogs a total of **23 breweries located in North America**. In contrast, the international presence is significantly smaller, with **Australia** housing exactly one brewery within the dataset.

California serves as a significant hub for activity; we have recorded a total of **1,192 transactions** involving root beer brands from this state. Across these California-based brands, the mean review count stands at **40.4 reviews per brand**.
