## Limitations & Caveats
This analysis is based strictly on the provided physical and status metrics. It does not account for total square footage (floor plate size), which is a critical missing data point for calculating exact employee capacity. Additionally, "under construction" status does not provide a specific "Topped Out" date or "Estimated Occupancy" date, which limits the precision of our immediate relocation timelines. Finally, the "on-hold" status does not specify if the delay is financial, legal, or structural, each of which would imply a different level of recovery risk.
