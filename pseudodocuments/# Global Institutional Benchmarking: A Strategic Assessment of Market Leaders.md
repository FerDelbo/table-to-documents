## Limitations & Caveats
This analysis is based on a static snapshot of fiscal data. It does not account for:
- **Intangible Assets**: Brand equity, patents, and intellectual property are not reflected in the "Assets_in_Billion" column but significantly influence "Market_Value."
- **Debt Load**: The table provides assets but not total liabilities, which limits our ability to calculate true net worth or debt-to-equity ratios.
- **Market Volatility**: Market Value is a fluctuating metric; the figures provided reflect a specific moment in time and may not capture recent shifts in investor sentiment or geopolitical events affecting the Oil and Gas or Banking sectors.
