## Limitations & Caveats
This analysis is based on a snapshot of physical release data. It does not account for:
- **Sales Volume**: We see the *presence* of formats but not their *performance*. A digipak in the US is only successful if it outsells the standard CD.
- **Price Points**: We lack the MSRP (Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price) for these regions, which would confirm if the US Digipak was indeed a "premium" play.
- **Marketing Spend**: We do not see the promotional budget allocated to each label, which might explain the US delay (e.g., waiting for a specific tour window).
- **Digital Leakage**: In July 2002, the "Napster effect" was real; a 15-day US delay may have resulted in significant unauthorized downloading that this table cannot quantify.
