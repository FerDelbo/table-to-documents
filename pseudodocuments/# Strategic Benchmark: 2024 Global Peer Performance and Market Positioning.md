Efficiency, defined here as Profit-to-Sales, is notably high in the Russian and Chinese state-linked sectors. Gazprom (Russia) shows a staggering 26.9% profit margin ($31.7B profit on $117.6B sales). ### What is the relationship between Sales and Market Value?
There is a loose correlation, but it is not absolute. Royal Dutch Shell has the highest sales ($470.2B) but only the fourth-highest Market Value ($227.6B). Conversely, ExxonMobil has lower sales than Shell ($433.5B) but a significantly higher Market Value ($407.4B). This indicates that the market rewards the *quality* and *profitability* of sales over the *quantity* of sales. We must prioritize high-margin revenue over top-line growth.
