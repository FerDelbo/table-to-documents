## Limitations & Caveats
- **Temporal Gaps**: The records do not specify the year of data collection. Economic and population figures are subject to annual fluctuations not captured in this static table.
- **Variable Definitions**: The term "Regional_Population" does not distinguish between registered residents (Hukou) and migratory populations, which may impact per-capita calculations.
- **Economic Scope**: GDP is a gross measure; it does not account for the internal composition of the economy (e.g., manufacturing vs. service sector), which would provide further context for the observed efficiency in Wuxi and Suzhou.
- **Geographic Constraints**: Area (square kilometers) and density data are missing from the current registry, preventing a full assessment of spatial efficiency.
